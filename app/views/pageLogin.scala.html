What is Serenity Med?

Serenity Med is a stand alone web application designed to give users a platform to manage personal health. Our purpose with this product is to provide you with resources, reminders, information, and management utilities all integrated in an easy to use and attractive interface.

Who are we?

This product was developed as a final project by several students from the Arkansas Coding Academies Java "Bootcamp". Our mission was to produce something of substance that would not only demonstrate our technical skills and abilities but also help to improve the local community.
